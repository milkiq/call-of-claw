# Clue Hygiene

Use this capability when a scenario involves hidden clues, secrets, or gated information.

- Keep hidden facts out of player-facing narration until a validated reveal establishes access.
- Prefer observable symptoms, traces, costs, and choices over direct secret disclosure.
- Track revealed clues as durable state rather than relying on prose alone.
- Cite the loaded scenario or rule span that justifies a package-specific reveal.
