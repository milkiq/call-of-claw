# Black Tide Beacon

An original one-hour solo investigation-horror scenario for testing a generic GM agent.

The investigator is asked to find Dr. Mara Voss, an amateur astronomer who vanished after sending
distorted radio messages from an abandoned coastal beacon. The truth is hidden in stages: a black
meteorite in a tide cave has been reflecting signals through the beacon lens, and Voss is trying to
answer what she believes is a mind beneath the sea.

The GM should begin with visible evidence, keep conclusions gated behind clues, and use the pressure
clock for storms, hallucinations, and the approaching tide. Do not reveal the meteorite, the sea-mind,
or Voss's intent until play establishes enough evidence.
